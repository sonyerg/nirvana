Krist Novoselic

Krist Anthony Novoselic (born May 16, 1965) is an American musician, politician and activist. Novoselic co-founded and played bass on every album for the rock band Nirvana.

After Nirvana disbanded in 1994 (following the death of Kurt Cobain), Novoselic formed Sweet 75 in 1995 and Eyes Adrift in 2002, releasing one album with each band.[2][3] From 2006 to 2009, he played in the punk rock band Flipper. In 2011, he contributed bass and accordion to the song "I Should Have Known" on the Foo Fighters' studio album Wasting Light. From 2017 to 2020, he played bass and accordion for the band Giants in the Trees.
